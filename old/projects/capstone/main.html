Swarm Carrier Capstone

As part of my senior engineering capstone project my friends and I are creating a large dodecacopter called the Carrier Drone that will transport six quadrotors called Swarm Drones. The goal of this project is to create a system capable of carrying the six Swarm Drones, deploying them mid-air, and then reintegrating them after a Search and Rescue mission.
